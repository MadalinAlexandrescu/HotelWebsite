About Hotel MB

Hotel MB offers ultimate comfort and luxury. This 4-storied hotel is a beautiful combination of traditional grandeur and modern facilities. The 255 exclusive guest rooms are furnished with a range of modern amenities such as television and internet access. International direct-dial phone and safe are also available in any of these rooms. Wake-up call facility is also available in these rooms.
